## Response:
The **Insta360 Nano** is **not officially supported by Matterport** as a compatible camera for capturing and uploading to the Matterport platform. Matterport requires specific camera capabilities, including depth data (or photogrammetry-compatible imagery), GPS stabilization, and app integration — features the Insta360 Nano does **not fully support**.

### Why Insta360 Nano is **not compatible**:

*   **Limited resolution**: While it captures 360° video and photos, the **image quality and resolution are not sufficient** for Matterport's spatial computing needs.
*   **No depth sensor**: Matterport relies on depth data (from LiDAR or structured light) or high-quality overlapping imagery to reconstruct 3D spaces.
*   **No SDK/API support**: The Nano doesn’t integrate with the Matterport Capture app, which is essential for upload and alignment.

### Compatible alternatives:

If you want to use a **360 camera with Matterport**, here are supported options:

*   **Ricoh Theta Z1**
*   **Ricoh Theta X**
*   **Insta360 ONE X2 / X3**
*   **Insta360 ONE RS 1-Inch 360 Edition**
*   **Matterport Pro2 / Pro3** (for high-precision capture)

### Recommendation:

If budget is a constraint and you’re aiming for cost-effective Matterport tours, the **Insta360 ONE X2/X3** is a good entry-level option that **does work** with Matterport’s platform, but delivers lower fidelity compared to the Pro2 or Pro3.
