## Prompt:
can gemini or chatgpt do it

## Response:
Yes — but with **important differences** between Gemini and ChatGPT:

* * *

✅ ChatGPT (OpenAI)
------------------

*   **Yes, ChatGPT can transcribe videos**, but only if:
    *   You upload the **audio/video file** (MP4, MP3, WAV, etc.) into the workspace.
    *   It then runs transcription using **Whisper** (OpenAI’s speech-to-text model).
*   **Limitations**:
    *   Not “unlimited” — depends on your plan (Plus has file size/time caps, and Enterprise/Team plans allow longer).
    *   More for **ad-hoc use** than bulk processing of a video library.
*   **Upside**:
    *   You can **immediately summarize, translate, or analyze** the transcript in ChatGPT, which saves a workflow step.
    *   Great if you want transcription + insights (not just raw text).

* * *

✅ Gemini (Google AI)
--------------------

*   Gemini (especially in **Google Workspace**) supports transcription via:
    *   **Gemini for Google Meet** → real-time meeting notes & summaries.
    *   **File uploads** (depending on Gemini Advanced or Workspace integrations) → you can upload video/audio and get transcripts.
*   **Limitations**:
    *   Similar to ChatGPT — capped by file size & subscription plan.
    *   Not a bulk/unlimited transcriber, more of a “productivity assistant.”
*   **Upside**:
    *   Tight integration with **Google Drive, Docs, and Meet**.
    *   Easy to push transcripts directly into Google Docs for editing.

* * *

🆚 Compared to dedicated transcription tools:
---------------------------------------------

*   **Descript, TurboScribe, Otter** → built for **bulk video/audio transcription**.
*   **ChatGPT/Gemini** → built for **multi-purpose AI** (they can transcribe, but that’s just one feature, not optimized for unlimited throughput).

* * *

### ⚖️ ROI view:

*   If you need **occasional transcription + summarization/translation** → ChatGPT or Gemini is perfect.
*   If you need **hundreds of hours per month** → a dedicated service (TurboScribe, Notta, or running Whisper locally) is far cheaper and scales better.
