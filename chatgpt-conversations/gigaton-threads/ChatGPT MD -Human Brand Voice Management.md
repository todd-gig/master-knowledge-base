Definition: Human Brand Voice Management System (HBVMS)
-------------------------------------------------------

A **Human Brand Voice Management System** is an operating system that **codifies, governs, and scales a brand’s human expression** across all channels—human and AI—so every outbound interaction is **consistent, intentional, compliant, and emotionally aligned** with the brand’s core beliefs and business objectives.

It is not content management.  
It is **behavioral control infrastructure** for how a brand _thinks, speaks, and responds_ at scale.

In executive terms:

> **HBVMS = Brand intent → enforced expression → measurable trust + conversion.**

* * *

Core Outcomes (Why It Exists)
-----------------------------

*   Eliminate brand drift across humans, vendors, and AI
*   Enable AI to speak _as the brand_, not “like marketing”
*   Increase trust velocity and signal coherence
*   Reduce revision cycles, legal risk, and reputational variance
*   Convert brand voice from a guideline into a **first-class system asset**

High ROI lever: once built, marginal cost of consistency → near zero.

3\. Scoring Logic (Deterministic)
=================================

### Per Dimension

```
dimension_score = (rules_passed / total_rules) * weight
```

### Total Score

```
total_score = Σ dimension_scores
```

### Release Logic

```
if hard_violation == true:
    reject
elif total_score >= channel_threshold:
    approve
elif total_score >= review_threshold:
    human_review
else:
    reject
```

4\. Drift Detection (Ongoing Control)
=====================================

Tracked over time:

*   Average score per channel
*   Variance from baseline
*   Belief alignment decay
*   Emotional volatility index

Trigger thresholds:

*   > 10% drop over 30 days → audit
*   Repeated near-threshold approvals → retraining required
