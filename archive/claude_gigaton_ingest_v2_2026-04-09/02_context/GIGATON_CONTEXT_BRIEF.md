# Gigaton Context Brief

## Company Definition
Gigaton is a human-centered AI startup studio, intelligence licensing company, and automated business-building ecosystem.

## Core Model
Gigaton captures intelligence once, structures it into reusable systems, then deploys it across clients, ventures, brands, sales flows, dashboards, and automated execution engines.

## Mission
Put technology to work for people by transforming human intelligence, creativity, relationships, and operational knowledge into ethical AI-powered systems that help individuals and organizations connect, create, thrive, and evolve.

## Strategic Positioning
Gigaton is not just a SaaS product. It is an AI operating system for business creation.

## Parent/Child Structure
- Gigaton = parent platform and intelligence kernel.
- TI Solutions / TI Life = execution and human operations layers.
- Liquifax, Carmen Beach, and future clients = product/client instances.

## Commercial Thesis
The highest-ROI path is repeatable automated packages that can be sold repeatedly with minimal incremental labor.
