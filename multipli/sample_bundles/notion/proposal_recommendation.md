## 3. Pilot structure (90-day design partner motion)

**Scope.** 50 enterprise-tier opportunities at Notion entering the financing-eligible cohort over 90 days, with explicit emphasis on opportunities where the buyer is considering Notion AI addon attach.

**Eligibility filter.** Annual contract value ≥ $20,000 (with or without AI addon); buyer entity passes Multipli credit screen; deal stage = "verbal_yes_pending_procurement" or equivalent; AI addon attach is one decision-tree branch within the financing offer.

**Offer at point of sale.** Notion sales rep introduces a Multipli-financed option as an alternative to annual prepay, framed as monthly or quarterly buyer payments over 12 months, with the financing fee borne by the buyer. AI addon is offered as a financing-included option (ramp from 0 seats at month 1 to full attach by month 6, all financed against the same buyer commitment) — this directly resolves the "AI addon value unproven at purchase time" objection.

## 5. Top buyer objections Multipli financing addresses

1. **"Annual prepay plus AI addon is a $30-50k upfront commitment our CFO won't approve."** Financing converts to monthly/quarterly with ramped AI attach.
2. **"We don't know if Notion AI is worth $8-10/seat for our use case yet."** Financing-with-ramp lets buyer start at low AI attach and grow over 12 months, paid as one financing commitment.
3. **"Seat count uncertainty in our hybrid/growing workforce makes a year of committed quantity risky."** Financing accommodates contract structures with step-up clauses.
4. **"Procurement requires net-30 or net-45 terms; we can't do annual prepay."** Financing fulfills extended-term request without Notion extending its own AR.
5. **"We already pay for Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace; adding Notion is hard to justify as another recurring line."** Financing converts the prepay-feeling-capex into manageable opex monthly draw, making side-by-side budget comparison easier.
6. **"Data migration from Confluence/SharePoint plus change management is a 6-month project; we can't pay full Notion year 1 while still ramping."** Financing aligns payment to actual ramp curve.

## 8. Risks (Notion-side)

- Buyer experience friction if Multipli credit-screening adds latency at close (mitigation: pre-qualify financing eligibility earlier in the funnel).
- Brand-association concern if a buyer defaults on Multipli financing — Notion is not the lender of record and is not liable, but the association may need messaging discipline.
- Sales-rep behavior: if reps overuse the financing offer instead of close-on-value, average discount or seat-count concessions could degrade. Mitigation: financing offered only in defined eligibility window.
- Notion AI attach-rate measurement noise: financed cohort may self-select for AI-curious buyers, inflating attach lift attribution. Mitigation: matched-cohort comparison rather than single-period attach delta.
