1. **"Annual prepay doesn't fit our budget cycle."** Financing converts to monthly/quarterly.
2. **"We don't know seat count for a full year."** Financing accommodates contract structures with usage-based step-up clauses.
3. **"Procurement requires net-30 or net-45 terms."** Financing fulfills extended-term request without Calendly extending its own AR.
4. **"CFO is pushing back on recurring software line items."** Financing converts capex-feeling prepay into manageable opex monthly draw.

## 8. Risks (Calendly-side)

- Buyer experience friction if Multipli credit-screening adds latency at close (mitigation: pre-qualify financing eligibility earlier in the funnel).
- Brand-association concern if a buyer defaults on Multipli financing — Calendly is not the lender of record and is not liable, but the association may need messaging discipline.
- Sales-rep behavior: if reps overuse the financing offer instead of close-on-value, average discount or seat-count concessions could degrade. Mitigation: financing offered only in defined eligibility window.
