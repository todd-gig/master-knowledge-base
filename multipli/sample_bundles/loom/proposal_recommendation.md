## 5. Top buyer objections Multipli financing addresses

1. **"Annual prepay PLUS AI addon doesn't fit our budget cycle."** Financing converts to monthly/quarterly and absorbs the AI addon uplift into a single line item.
2. **"We don't know seat count for a full year — async video adoption varies by team."** Financing accommodates contract structures with usage-based step-up clauses.
3. **"Procurement requires net-30 or net-45 terms, plus extra video-data-residency review."** Financing fulfills extended-term request without Loom extending its own AR; video compliance review can proceed in parallel.
4. **"CFO is pushing back on recurring software line items, and AI line items get extra scrutiny."** Financing converts capex-feeling prepay into manageable opex monthly draw and de-emphasizes the AI addon as a discrete budget battle.
5. **"We already have Microsoft Stream/Zoom Clips bundled free."** Financing makes the differentiated-value sell easier by removing the headline price comparison and shifting the decision to monthly cash flow.

## 8. Risks (Loom-side / Atlassian-side)

- Buyer experience friction if Multipli credit-screening adds latency at close (mitigation: pre-qualify financing eligibility earlier in the funnel; coordinate with Loom's existing video-data-residency review window to absorb credit review in parallel).
- Brand-association concern if a buyer defaults on Multipli financing — Loom (and Atlassian) is not the lender of record and is not liable, but the association may need messaging discipline.
- Sales-rep behavior: if reps overuse the financing offer instead of close-on-value, average discount or seat-count concessions could degrade. Mitigation: financing offered only in defined eligibility window.
- Atlassian post-acquisition packaging risk: if Loom is repackaged into an Atlassian premium tier mid-pilot, scenario math shifts materially. Mitigation: scope pilot to current standalone Loom SKUs with a re-baseline clause if packaging changes.
- AI addon attach-rate inflation perception: if financing materially lifts AI attach rate, Atlassian finance may want to renegotiate the AI addon list price; mitigation — agree pilot data is not used to drive list-price changes within pilot window.
