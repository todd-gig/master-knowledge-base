------------------------------------

This operating model is engineered for **maximum coverage, minimum capability gaps, and scalable human-supervised intelligence development**.

Design logic
------------

**Objective:** create an organization that can:

1.  Solve as many business problems as possible at granular resolution.
2.  Convert vague executive intent into operational execution.
3.  Build a compounding system of **human supervisors + specialist operators + agentic tooling**.
4.  Ensure every role can be decomposed into:
    *   skills
    *   talents
    *   experience
    *   qualifications
    *   supervision requirements
    *   measurable outputs
    *   promotable successor paths

------------------------------

*   **1 CEO**
*   **6 C-suite leaders**
*   **Each executive has 7 direct reports**
*   **Each of those has 3–5 direct reports**
*   **Each of those has up to 3 direct reports**

This yields a structure that is:

*   broad enough for enterprise coverage
*   shallow enough for control
*   modular enough for future automation
*   suitable for mapping into a skills ontology and certification system
