# Margin Alert Rules

## Alert Conditions
- Gross margin below threshold
- Repeated discounting beyond policy
- Support cost exceeds expected range
- Delivery hours exceed scoped range
- Bundle sold below target contribution

## Required Actions
- Trigger pricing review
- Trigger delivery review
- Trigger bundle restructuring review
- Escalate to owner when threshold breached repeatedly
