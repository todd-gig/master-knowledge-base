# Objective
Measure the business delta between Claude, Python, and Hybrid execution.

# Benchmark Dimensions
- unit cost
- latency
- quality score
- agreement rate
- exception rate
- human review burden
- codification savings potential

# Core Use
Benchmark evidence should drive:
- routing policy updates,
- codification priority,
- release approvals,
- architecture decisions.

# Rule
Do not optimize for lowest cost alone.
Optimize for:
- lowest viable cost,
- acceptable quality,
- sustainable scale,
- future margin.
