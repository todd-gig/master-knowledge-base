## Stripe payment architecture (user clarification)

**Stripe is owned by Gigaton (org-level platform account).** Tenants integrate their own Stripe accounts via **Stripe Connect** to accept payments + receive payouts. Payout configuration varies per tenant agreement.

**Architectural implication:**

- Platform Stripe account = `Gigaton, Inc.` — receives all charges initially, takes platform fee, routes remainder to connected accounts.
- Tenants onboard via **Stripe Connect Express** (recommended — Stripe-hosted KYC, fastest time-to-revenue) or **Custom** (platform owns full UX, more work) per tenant tier.
- Per-tenant payout rules stored in `connect_accounts` table: split percentage, payout schedule (daily/weekly/monthly), currency, hold periods.
- Carmen Beach properties: bookings hit Gigaton Stripe → Connect routes property-owner share + affiliate commission + retains platform fee.
- Ti Solutions: their own Connect account, money flows to their merchant of record, Gigaton platform fee deducted.
- Affiliates: Connect Express accounts receive commission payouts on the schedule defined in `affiliate_org_tier_policy.md`.

**Plan deltas:**

- Day 4 task 4.2 ("Stripe products + prices") becomes "**Gigaton Stripe Connect Platform setup** + Express onboarding flow + per-tenant `connect_accounts` schema".
- Day 5 task 5.1 ("billing dashboard cards") adds a **Connect onboarding card** (KYC status, payouts pending, last payout, account balance).
- Day 6 affiliates payout test becomes a Connect-Express-routed transfer test, not a manual payout.

This raises Day 4–6 complexity — Stripe Connect is more work than vanilla Stripe Checkout. Mitigation: ship **Express** mode for fastest path, defer Custom for Cycle 2.
