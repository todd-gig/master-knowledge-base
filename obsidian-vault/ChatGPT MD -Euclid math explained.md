**Definition**

1\. **Straight-Line Distance (Core Euclid → Coordinate Geometry)**
------------------------------------------------------------------

Euclid defines distance using straight lines as the shortest path. In coordinates, that becomes Pythagoras.

Two points:  
 $A(x_1, y_1)$

$$
AB = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}
