## Prompt:
what is the definition of a virtuous cycle

## Response:
A **virtuous cycle** is a **self-reinforcing loop of positive outcomes** where each beneficial result creates conditions that lead to further improvement.

Formally:

> A virtuous cycle occurs when a system’s feedback mechanisms amplify desirable effects, leading to sustained growth, stability, or success over time.

Example:

*   Higher customer satisfaction → more referrals → higher revenue → better products → even higher satisfaction.

The opposite is a **vicious cycle**, where negative feedback reinforces decline.
