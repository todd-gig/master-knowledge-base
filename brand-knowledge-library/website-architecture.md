## Page Structure

### 1. HOME
**URL:** turtleisland.life

**Content flow:**
1. **Greeting** — "Welcome to Ti Life"
2. **Talent Purpose** — "To give people ability to earn more learning to work remote for better matched clients"
3. **Pillars of Ti Life** — Time, Work, Fun, Travel, Education, Giving Back, Family
4. **Qualification Process** — Steps to join
5. **Teams List** — Available departments
6. **Island Life Section** — Koh Tao lifestyle content

### 2. TEAM ROLE TEMPLATE PAGE
**URL:** turtleisland.life/[team-name]

**Content per role:**
- Team title and mission
- Team goals
- Role descriptions
- Qualifications required
- Certifications available
- Compensation matrix
- CTA: Apply or qualify for paid OJE

### 3. OUR SERVICES
**URL:** turtleisland.life/services

**Categories:**
- **People** — Staffing, Digital Marketing, Channel Management, Local Marketing, Remote Onsite Management
- **Process** — Systems design, workflow optimization
- **Technology** — Software development, automation
- **Data** — Analytics, reporting, insights

### 4. JOIN US / APPLY FOR OJE
**URL:** turtleisland.life/join

**Conversion funnel:**
1. Greeting — Welcome
2. Introduction — Who/what/values, matching talent with roles
3. Presell — Hiring process, steps to success
4. Sale — Role descriptions, compensation, benefits
5. Sale Close — Team requirements
6. CTA — Complete application
7. Upsell — Schedule to learn about alternative roles
8. Crossell — Suggest better-fit roles for near-misses
9. Downsell — Entry-level starting point for willing workers
10. Social — Follow, learn more, future notifications

### 5. OUR STORY
**URL:** turtleisland.life/story

**Sections:**
- Who: Ti Companies overview
- What: Services and value proposition
- Why: Koh Tao and the #onsiteRemote model
- Where: International operations + Koh Tao hub
- Giving Back: Coral, turtles, trash, school
- Island Life Video

### 6. LANDING PAGES

| Page | Content |
|------|---------|
| Retreats | Coworking, villa, food, diving pricing; Stripe booking |
| Investor | Investment opportunity overview |
| CxGuy Personal | Todd Nelson profile + connect |

### 7. TALENT MANAGEMENT
**URL:** turtleisland.life/talent

**Features:**
- Applicant generation
- Data filtering
- Evaluation processes
- Benchmark games (gamified assessment)

### 8. INDIVIDUAL PROFILE PAGES
Each employee has a unique page linked to their LinkedIn profile.

### 9. FOOTER
- Newsletter signup
- Social media follow links
- Link to Ti Solutions (turtleisland.solutions)
