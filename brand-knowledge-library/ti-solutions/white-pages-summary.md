# Ti Solutions — White Pages Summary

## Overview
The Ti Solutions White Pages is the primary client-facing company overview document, shared with all candidates and prospective clients as a comprehensive introduction to the Ti Companies ecosystem.

## Key Sections

### Company Introduction
- Ti Solutions provides Fortune 500-level consulting expertise to SaaS companies and startups
- Established to profitably provide service-centric clients access to Tier 1 talent
- Emphasis on process design as the foundation of performance

### Service Architecture
Organized into four pillars:
1. **People** — Staffing, recruitment, workforce management, gamification
2. **Process** — Systems design, workflow optimization, automation
3. **Technology** — Software development (Rails, React, Python), infrastructure
4. **Data** — Analytics, reporting, behavioral science, feedback loops

### Client Engagement Model
- **Discovery** — Identify cost-saving and unrealized profit opportunities
- **Architecture** — Design efficient software, business, and interaction management systems
- **Implementation** — Build and deploy solutions
- **Optimization** — Ongoing management, QA/QC, reporting

### Contact Center Capabilities
- **Inbound:** Support, service, IT help desk, retention, sales
- **Outbound:** Prospecting, lead management, appointment scheduling, sales
- **Digital:** Chat, SMS, email, social media
- **Compliance:** HIPAA, PCI certified processes
- **Languages:** English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Thai

### Sales Cycle Design
- CRM strategy and implementation (Zoho primary)
- Lead-to-LVC (Lifetime Value of Customer) tracking
- Automated follow-up sequences
- Chatbot integration
- Dynamic scripting

### Marketing Capabilities
- **Digital:** Google AdWords, Facebook Ads, Twitter Ads, LinkedIn Ads
- **Social:** Multi-stream social media management with automated QA
- **Direct:** Email drip campaigns, direct mail
- **Data:** Data purchasing, validation, impression marketing
- **Offline:** Billboards, penny savers, local marketing

### Technology Standards
- Ruby on Rails (bbatsov standards)
- React JS (Airbnb JS style guide)
- Python (PEP-0008)
- PostgreSQL / NGINX
- REST APIs / JSON

### Communication Expectations
- Clear, concise, and direct communication
- Blunt communication is acceptable, not rude
- Transparency in reporting and progress
- Properly designed reporting gives the clearest picture
