# Brand Knowledge Library

Structured brand intelligence for the Ti Companies ecosystem, extracted from Google Drive documents, Zoho forms, and internal brand guidelines.

## Companies

| Company | Domain | Role |
|---------|--------|------|
| **Ti Solutions** | turtleisland.solutions | B2B consulting, BPO, CX design, software development |
| **Ti Life** | turtleisland.life | Talent lifestyle management, concierge services, retreats |
| **Total Interactions** | — | Interaction/channel management division |

**Owner:** Todd Nelson ("CxGuy") — todd.cx@turtleisland.solutions

## Structure

```
brand-knowledge-library/
├── brand-identity.md          # Colors, typography, visual identity
├── brand-voice.md             # Tone, messaging guidelines, communication style
├── company-profile.md         # Mission, vision, purpose, structure
├── service-catalog.md         # All service offerings across Ti companies
├── talent-acquisition.md      # OJE program, roles, hiring funnel, email templates
├── competitive-landscape.md   # Market positioning, competitors
├── partnerships.md            # Affiliate agreements, solution partners
├── website-architecture.md    # Ti Life website structure and content plan
├── ti-life/                   # Ti Life specific assets
│   └── social-content.md      # LinkedIn content, emoji library, social copy
└── ti-solutions/              # Ti Solutions specific assets
    └── white-pages-summary.md # Client-facing company overview
```

## Usage

This library is designed to be referenced by Claude and other AI agents to automatically generate brand-aligned content including:
- Marketing copy and social media posts
- Email templates and candidate communications
- Sales proposals and client presentations
- Website content and landing pages
- Brand-consistent design briefs
