# Ti Companies — Competitive Landscape

## Market Context

- Rising US minimum wage + healthcare costs making local talent acquisition cost-prohibitive
- COVID forced remote work adoption; ongoing onsite-vs-remote debate
- Ti's answer: **#onsiteRemote** — physical presence in desirable locations
- Koh Tao as ideal hub: training culture, affordable living, 500MB internet, "world's highest concentration of certified emergency professionals"

## Key Differentiators

1. **Concierge lifestyle management** — Housing, food, diving, wellness included
2. **#onsiteRemote model** — Best of both worlds: team colocation in paradise
3. **Social responsibility monetized** — Happiness as a business model, not a perk
4. **Fortune 500 expertise at SME pricing** — $3,500–$5,000/month all-inclusive
5. **Koh Tao location** — Low cost of living, high quality of life, training culture
6. **B Corp aspiration** — Employee-owned, socially responsible structure
7. **Full-stack services** — BPO + software + marketing + CX in one provider
