## Vision, Mission, Purpose

### Ti Solutions

**Vision:**
> "To act as an example of corporate accountability by monetizing social responsibility through talent experience value."

**Mission:**
> "We help clients identify cost-saving and unrealized profit opportunities as we assist them in architecting and developing efficient software, business, and client interaction management systems that simplify customer interaction management operations and create a stable foundation for growth at an increased profit margin."

**Purpose:**
> "Ti Solutions was established to profitably provide service centric SaaS & Startup clients access to Tier 1 talent capable of establishing industry service expectations."

**Goal:**
> "To prove happier people, create happier customers by providing talent capable of helping our clients establish industry service expectations. To provide SME & startup clients access to Tier 1 talent capable of producing better results at a lower cost."

### Ti Life

**Tagline:**
> "WE'RE PROVING HAPPINESS IS PROFITABLE"

**Mission:**
> "To provide Ti Talent access to Ti Life expectations, enjoy their client interactions, roles, responsibilities, and provide exceptional talent the ability to work for clients they love on projects they like that make them happy."

**Establishment Purpose:**
> "Ti Life was established to act as an example of corporate accountability by monetizing social responsibility through talent life experience improvement."

### Founding Rationale (Both Brands)

> "Ti was established to act as an example of a socially responsible company proving happier people create happier customers — by monetizing the value of talent quality of life improvement."
