# Ti Life — Talent Acquisition

## OJE Program (On-the-Job Evaluation)

### Overview
Paid program qualifying candidates for onsite Koh Tao, Thailand placement.

### Requirements
- 100 hours of productive remote call center agent hours to qualify
- 300 hours remote = qualifies for island onsite
- "Earn your ticket" pathway: 300 hours remote + save for plane + first 30 days

### Benefits Upon Qualification
- 30 days accommodation
- Transportation (moped rental)
- Travel allowance
- Visa support
- Company insurance
- Up to 10,000 THB arrival bonus
- Food allowance

## Hiring Funnel

### Stages
1. **Application** — Zoho Forms or Upwork proposal
2. **White Pages Review** — Candidate reads company overview
3. **Screening Interview** — With Zeph (Chief Talent Officer)
4. **Second Interview** — With Todd (CxGuy) — focuses on 1/3/5 year goals
5. **Offer** — Via PandaDoc
6. **Onboarding** — Software setup (Slack, Zoom, GSuite, Loom, ClickUp, Facebook)
7. **Island Certification** — Onsite qualification at Koh Tao

### Scheduling
- **Zeph:** calendly.com/turtleisland_talent/interview
- **Todd:** calendly.com/todd-cx/interview

## Email Templates (Zoho Talent Emails)

### 1. Upwork — No HR (Direct Interview)
- **Trigger:** Upwork freelancer selected for non-HR role
- **Audience:** Short-term/auxiliary talent
- **Action:** Schedule tech interview with Todd
- **Signed by:** Todd

### 2. Upwork Proposal Selected
- **Trigger:** Upwork candidate selected
- **Action:** Share white pages, request application completion
- **Signed by:** Zeph

### 3. Selected to Interview from Application
- **Trigger:** Application reviewed, candidate selected
- **Subject:** "SCHEDULE your INTERVIEW with TI Life 🐢🏝"
- **Action:** Schedule screening with Zeph
- **Signed by:** "At work for you, Zeph, Chief Talent Officer, Ti Life"

### 4. NOT Selected to Interview
- **Trigger:** Application reviewed, candidate declined
- **Tone:** Polite but firm; no encouragement to reapply
- **Signed by:** "Kind regards, Zeph"

### 5. Post-Talent Interview — Selected for Todd
- **Trigger:** Zeph interview passed
- **Subject:** "Schedule your meeting with CxGuy"
- **Content:** Prep tip about 1/3/5 year goals
- **Signed by:** "At work for you, Zeph"

### 6. Post-Talent Interview — Not Selected for Todd
- **Trigger:** Zeph interview, not advancing
- **Content:** Decline with 90-day active pipeline mention
- **Signed by:** "Kind regards, Zeph"

### 7. Redirect to Another Role
- **Trigger:** Candidate better suited for different position
- **Action:** Direct to alternative role decision owner

### 8. Post-Todd Interview — Offer Extended
- **Trigger:** Todd interview passed
- **Content:** Congratulations + onboarding process + PandaDoc offer letter link
- **Software setup list:** Slack, Zoom, GSuite, Loom, ClickUp, Facebook

### 9. Post-Todd Interview — Not Now (Keep Warm)
- **Trigger:** Near-miss candidate
- **Content:** Keep warm, future consideration

### 10. Post-Todd Interview — No Offer
- **Trigger:** Final decline
- **Content:** Firm close

### 11. Role Opens for Pre-Qualified Candidate
- **Trigger:** New role matches previously interviewed talent
- **Content:** Personalized re-engagement referencing prior interview month

### 12. Sorry for Delay
- **Trigger:** Candidate delayed in qualification process
- **Subject:** "Ti Companies Interaction Experience Team Test Task"
- **Action:** Complete Zoho qualification form
- **Signed by:** Zeph
