This Privacy Policy describes how Gigaton AI collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you access the Gigaton platform, the website at `gigaton.ai`, the application at `app.gigaton.ai`, or the API at `api.gigaton.ai` (collectively, the "**Service**").

## 3. What Personal Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information you provide directly

- **Account registration data**: name, email address, password (hashed), company name, role, phone number (optional).
- **Billing data**: billing address, payment-card last-4 + expiry + brand (collected and stored by Stripe — we never store full card numbers).
- **Profile and brand setup data**: brand voice descriptors, business description, communication preferences.
- **Support and communications**: messages you send to support, feedback you submit.

### 3.2 Information collected automatically

- **Usage data**: pages and features accessed, queries submitted, generated artifacts, session timestamps, error logs.
- **Device and connection data**: IP address, browser type/version, operating system, device identifiers, referrer URL.
- **Cookies and similar technologies**: see our Cookie Notice in Section 12.

### 3.3 Information from third-party Connectors

When you connect a third-party service (Google Drive, Gmail, Calendar, WhatsApp via Twilio, Stripe, etc.), we receive the data covered by the OAuth scopes or API credentials you grant. This may include:

- **Google Drive / Google Workspace**: file contents, file metadata, folder structure (scoped to what you authorize).
- **Gmail**: email contents and metadata (scoped to what you authorize — typically read-only access to labeled threads).
- **Google Calendar**: event data, attendee info, availability.
- **WhatsApp Business via Twilio**: phone numbers, message contents, delivery status, conversation history.
- **Stripe**: customer records, transaction history (read-only for cost-attribution analytics).

### 3.4 Information generated by the Service

- **AI Generated Output**: drafts, summaries, recommendations, scoring, classifications produced by AI Subprocessors.
- **Interaction telemetry**: PPIM-class fields per the [Universal Connector Hub architecture](../../docs/architecture/universal_connector_hub_architecture.md) — interaction class, cost, revenue attribution, predictability score, brand dimension.
- **Logs and audit trails**: connector activity, sign-off events, security events.
