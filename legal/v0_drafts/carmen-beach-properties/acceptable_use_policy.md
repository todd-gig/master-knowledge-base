# Carmen Beach Properties — Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-26
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-25
**Contact:** concierge@carmenbeach.com (TODO: confirm)

This Acceptable Use Policy ("**AUP**") governs your use of Carmen Beach's Services, including (a) the website and owner portal, (b) the WhatsApp concierge channel, (c) physical use of any Property during an authorized Stay, and (d) any related services. It is incorporated by reference into the [Terms of Service](terms_of_service.md). Capitalized terms have the meanings given there.

This AUP applies to **Guests, Owners, and visitors**.

Violation may result in immediate termination of a Stay, revocation of booking, forfeiture of payments, charges against the security deposit, deletion of account, and/or legal proceedings.

## 1. General Conduct

You agree to use the Services and any Property:

- **Lawfully** — in compliance with all applicable laws (Mexican federal, state of Quintana Roo, municipal; U.S. state and federal where relevant; international travel and customs law).
- **Respectfully** — toward staff, contractors, neighbors, other Guests, and the local community.
- **As intended** — for the residential or recreational purpose stated in your booking or for the property-management purpose stated in your PMA.

## 2. Prohibited Uses — General

### 2.1 Illegal activity

You may not use the Services or any Property to:

(a) Violate any applicable law, including but not limited to drug trafficking, prostitution, human trafficking, weapons offenses, or commercial fraud.
(b) Possess, manufacture, or distribute controlled substances (Mexican `Ley General de Salud` provisions); a small amount of personal-use cannabis where decriminalized in Mexico does not constitute manufacturing/distribution but may still implicate house rules.
(c) Engage in unauthorized commercial filming, photography for sale, or commercial events at the Property.
(d) Engage in money laundering, tax evasion, or sanctions evasion.
(e) Operate a brothel or sex-work establishment.
(f) Engage in human trafficking, exploitation of minors, or any conduct prohibited by 18 U.S.C. § 2252A or Mexican equivalents.

### 2.2 Property damage and misuse

(a) Damage, destroy, or remove Property fixtures, furnishings, or inventory.
(b) Hold parties, weddings, large gatherings, or events exceeding the booking's stated occupancy or the Property's maximum occupancy.
(c) Use the Property as a film set, photo studio for commercial use, podcast/recording studio for distribution, or other commercial use without prior written consent.
(d) Set unauthorized fires, use unauthorized fireworks, or violate Property fire-safety rules.
(e) Bring pets without prior consent and the appropriate pet fee.
(f) Smoke (including vaping and cannabis) inside the Property where prohibited by house rules.

### 2.3 Disturbance and quiet enjoyment

(a) Violate stated quiet hours (typically 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM local time, or as posted in the welcome packet).
(b) Use audio equipment, generators, or vehicles in a manner that disturbs neighbors.
(c) Engage in conduct that draws law-enforcement responses to the Property (multiple incidents may result in immediate termination of the Stay).

### 2.4 Safety and occupancy

(a) Exceed the maximum occupancy stated in the listing — overage rates may apply where authorized, but unauthorized overage is a violation.
(b) Bring weapons, explosives, or hazardous materials onto the Property.
(c) Allow access to anyone whose stay was not disclosed at booking (no "sneaky stays" — third-party occupants who are not on the booking are violations).
(d) Sublet, transfer, or re-list the Property on any third-party platform.

## 3. Communications and Concierge Use

### 3.1 Use of WhatsApp concierge

The Carmen Beach WhatsApp channel is for Stay-related communications, booking inquiries, and concierge support. You may not:

(a) Use the concierge channel to send spam, marketing, or unsolicited commercial messages.
(b) Send harassing, threatening, sexually explicit, or hateful messages to staff or AI assistants.
(c) Attempt to manipulate the AI assistant to generate prohibited content (see Section 4).
(d) Impersonate other Guests, staff, or third parties.

Reasonable use; abusive use will result in concierge-channel suspension and may escalate to Stay termination.

### 3.2 Reviews

You may submit reviews of your Stay; reviews must be honest, accurate, and consistent with applicable consumer-protection laws (no fake reviews, no extortion-based reviews threatening negative review unless demands are met).

## 4. AI Misuse (Specifically Applicable to Concierge Bot)

You may not use the Carmen Beach AI-assisted services to:

(a) Generate content that violates Section 2 (illegal, harmful, harassing).
(b) Probe, jailbreak, or extract system prompts from the AI assistant.
(c) Use the AI assistant to perform tasks unrelated to your Stay or Property (e.g., asking it to write a college essay, generate malware, or produce general-purpose content unrelated to Carmen Beach).
(d) Misrepresent AI-generated content as authored by a named Carmen Beach human staff member.

We may rate-limit, suspend, or terminate access to the AI assistant for misuse without affecting your underlying Stay rights (e.g., you can still call human concierge).

(a) List a property for which the Owner does not have title or proper authorization.

(d) Bypass Carmen Beach's exclusivity arrangement under the PMA by separately listing on OTAs.
(e) Refuse Guests based on protected characteristics (Mexican constitutional Art. 1 + US fair-housing analogs where applicable) — guest-acceptance decisions must be on lawful, neutral criteria.
(f) Submit fake reviews or pay third parties to do so.
