Customer represents and warrants that:

(a) Customer's contact lists and target audiences were obtained lawfully and Customer has the right to direct outreach to those individuals;
(b) Customer has obtained all required consents for SMS, voice, WhatsApp, and (where applicable) email outreach — including TCPA prior express written consent for telemarketing calls/texts, CAN-SPAM commercial-email compliance, PROFECO-administered consumer-protection compliance for Mexican-resident contacts, and LFPDPPP consent for Mexican personal data;

## 2. Prohibited Uses — General

Customer may not engage Ti Solutions to, and Ti Solutions will not perform on Customer's behalf:

### 2.1 Illegal activity

(a) Any outreach, content, or service prohibited by US, Mexican, state, provincial, or local law;
(b) Money laundering, sanctions evasion, terrorism financing, securities fraud, or commercial fraud;
(c) Distribution of child sexual abuse material, non-consensual intimate imagery, or content depicting violence against minors;
(d) Activity facilitating human trafficking or exploitation.

### 2.2 Spam, scraping, abuse

(a) Bulk unsolicited messaging in violation of CAN-SPAM, TCPA, REPEP, LFPDPPP, or analogous laws;
(b) Use of purchased / scraped / harvested contact lists without verifiable consent;
(c) Falsification of sender identity, subject lines, or routing;
(d) Engagement levels (open rates, complaint rates) that trigger Twilio / Google / Meta WhatsApp deliverability penalties — this can compromise the entire Gigaton Platform and may result in immediate engagement suspension;
(e) Scraping or extracting data from third-party websites/services in violation of those services' terms.

### 2.3 Credential stuffing, account abuse

Customer may not direct Ti Solutions to engage in credential-stuffing, password-cracking, or other authentication-attack activity against any system.

### 2.4 Abusive content

(a) Generation or distribution of harassing, threatening, defamatory, hateful, or obscene content;
(b) Deepfakes, voice clones, or synthetic-identity impersonations without clear written consent of the subject + appropriate labeling;
(c) Coordinated harassment, doxxing, swatting, or comparable conduct.

### 2.5 AI misuse

(a) Use of AI to generate disinformation, manipulative political content, fraudulent claims, or fake reviews;
(b) Mass-generation of low-quality SEO/content-farm material;
(c) Generation of CBRN / malware / harm-causing tools;
(d) Use of AI to circumvent content-moderation systems on Connector platforms.

### 2.6 Service interference

Probing or attacking the Gigaton Platform, the Ti Solutions delivery infrastructure, or third-party systems used in the engagement.

## 3. Prohibited Industries / Use Cases (Without Pre-Approval)

Without prior written approval from Ti Solutions, Customer may not engage Ti Solutions for outreach, marketing, sales operations, or interaction management in the following categories:

- **Adult content / sex work**;
- **Online gambling** in unlicensed jurisdictions;
- **Cannabis** outside fully-legal jurisdictions and licensed actors;
- **Cryptocurrency / virtual assets** unless Customer is properly licensed and disclosed;

- **Political campaign messaging** (TCPA + state political-advertising rules apply);

## 4. Prohibited Instructions

Customer may not instruct Ti Solutions to:

(a) Misrepresent itself as a different entity;
(b) Send messages with deceptive "from" lines, deceptive subject lines, or deceptive landing pages;
(c) Promise outcomes (financial returns, regulatory approvals) without substantiation;
(d) Use scarcity / urgency claims that are not factually true;
(e) Bypass opt-outs received from prior recipients;
(f) Re-engage individuals who have opted out, requested deletion, or filed PROFECO/state-AG complaints related to Customer's prior outreach;
(g) Process personal data of EU/EEA/UK/CH residents during the v1 beta period;
(h) Use Customer's relationship with Ti Solutions to launder messages that Customer's own infrastructure has been blocked from delivering.

## 5. Recipient Protections

Ti Solutions retains the right to (and will, regardless of Customer instructions):

(a) Honor opt-outs received from recipients (STOP / UNSUBSCRIBE / "darme de baja");
(b) Decline to send messages that would violate applicable law in our judgment;
(c) Insert legally-required disclosures (sender identification, opt-out mechanism, postal address per CAN-SPAM);
(d) Throttle or pause campaigns triggering complaint-rate thresholds;
(e) Report violations to law enforcement where required by law.
