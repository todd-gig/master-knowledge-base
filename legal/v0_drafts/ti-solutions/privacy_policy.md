## 8. Controller vs. Processor — Outbound Campaign Data

When Ti Solutions runs outbound campaigns on behalf of a customer (e.g., LinkedIn outbound, cold email, WhatsApp outreach to vendors-of-vendors), the **customer is the controller** and **Ti Solutions is the processor**:

- The **customer** determines the purposes (their lead-generation strategy), the means (target audience, message content approved by customer), and the lawful basis (typically legitimate interest under US frameworks; consent / contractual necessity under MX LFPDPPP).
- **Ti Solutions** processes the data only per the customer's documented instructions, under the [Data Processing Addendum](data_processing_addendum.md).
- **Recipients of outbound campaigns** should direct privacy inquiries to the customer (the controller). If contacted, Ti Solutions will forward to the customer within 5 business days.

This division is critical for TCPA, CAN-SPAM, and PROFECO-administered Mexican consumer-protection compliance, all of which place primary obligations on the controller.
